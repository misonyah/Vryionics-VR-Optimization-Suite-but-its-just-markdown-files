# Code of Conduct

We follow a short and direct standard.

**Be civil. Be constructive. Don't punch down.**

That's it. The maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, close issues, ban participants, or revert merges from contributors who repeatedly violate this. We don't owe anyone a platform if they're consistently nasty in our threads.

If you've experienced or witnessed conduct here that you think crosses the line, email **thegaminglemon256@gmail.com**. We won't surveil discussions for thoughtcrimes; we will respond seriously to direct reports.

This is not a substitute for the Contributor Covenant — if you'd like the formality of a standard, [theirs](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) is well-written and we don't object to it being applied alongside this one. But for our day-to-day expectations: civil, constructive, no punching down.
